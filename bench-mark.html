Benchmark Definitions

Active Clients

These are clients that require direct attention from advisers in the business, whether it be face to face consultations, phone calls and/or strategy reviews. These clients may be charged ongoing fees for the management and review of their portfolios. Clients managed solely by support staff, without direct contact with advisers are not considered actively managed clients.

Passive Clients

These are clients that require direct attention from advisers in the business, whether it be face to face consultations, phone calls and/or strategy reviews. These clients may be charged ongoing fees for the management and review of their portfolios. Clients managed solely by support staff, without direct contact with advisers are not considered actively managed clients.

New Revenue, New Clients

This represents revenue streams generated from clients acquired during the 2005/06 financial year. This may arise from planning fees, placement of new funds and pre payments for ongoing services and/or advice.

New Revenue, Actively Managed Clients

This represents new revenue streams generated during the 2005/06 financial year for existing, actively managed clients. This may arise from the placement of new funds, entry fee commissions resulting from a switch of funds and/or a new service offering.

Recurring Revenue

This represents recurring revenue streams generated during the last financial year. The most common stream is trail commissions and annual review fees. It does not include revenue arising from either new clients and/or new funds being introduced.

Full Time Equivalent (FTE)

A full time equivalent (FTE) is defined as one person working 5 days per week in the business, 100% dedicated on activities directly associated with the business. For example, a part time adviser working 3 days per week on average, is expressed as 0.6 of an FTE. An employee working 4 days per week and another working 2 days per week, equate to 1.2 FTE's in total.
An owner who works, on average, 3 days per week in the business and dedicates the remaining 2 days to other business activities / interests is defined a 0.6 of an FTE, even though they may be drawing a salary equivalent to a full time manager or adviser.
